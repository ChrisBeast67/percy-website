🤺 Fencing

About Fencing

Fencing is a group of three related combat sports. The three disciplines (Foil, Epée, and Sabre) all use a variety of flexible steel blades, and are practiced competitively.

The sport began in Spain and has been described as "the art of the sword."

🎯 The Three Weapons

Foil: A light thrusting weapon with a flexible blade. Only touches with the point.

Épée: A heavy thrusting weapon. The entire body is a valid target.

Sabre: A light cutting and thrusting weapon. Targets are above the waist.

🏆 Famous Fencers

• Valentina Vezzali (Italy) - 6 Olympic gold medals

• Ibtihaj Muhammad (USA) - First Muslim-American woman to win a medal

• Edoardo Mangiarotti (Italy) - 13 Olympic medals

🛡️ Protective Gear

Fencers wear protective gear including:

• Mask - Protects the face

• Glove - Protects the sword hand

• Jacket - Protective outer layer

• Breeches - Knee-length pants
